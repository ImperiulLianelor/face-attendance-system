Core Functionalities:
User Registration and Authentication: Admins can register students or employees, including uploading photos for the facial recognition database.
Face Recognition Attendance: The system captures and recognizes faces in real-time or from photos to mark attendance automatically.
Attendance Dashboard: An interface showing daily, weekly, and monthly attendance statistics. Admins can generate and export reports.
Technology Stack:
Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (React for dynamic Ul)
Backend: Python
Face Recognition: OpenCV and Dlib or a pre-trained model from a library like Face Recognition (built on top of Dlib)
Database: PostgreSQL
